Business Models for Smart Things

Three Stages of Smart Things

  • Solutions

    include modules, app, server and etc.

  • Mid-product

    Products without brand.

  • End-product

    Products with brand.

Solutions

Solutions

The solution will include:

  • module

    Auth Cp or Auth Token inside with explicit function, e.g. bulb

  • app

    An app with HomeKit SDK is necessary for bi-cloud solution

  • server

    To apply Auth Token, and other services for bi-cloud service

Solutions

Propeller

The solution provider

Core Demands

I. Easy and intuitive to estimate the solutions.
II. Help the manufactory to make desicion quickly.

Output

The solutions that can be introduced and expierenced by many manufacotries.

Solutions

Problems

  • Solution providers can't deliver the modules without the help from manufactory
  • Manufactory need to spend much time and money before estimating the solution and deciding whether to start or not

Solutions

Suggestions

  • Is it possible to provide a special type of product plan, maybe called "solution plan"
    • Based on chip, authentication type, communication type, etc.
    • Not based on category, HomeKit services, etc.
  • Development licensee may apply for this kind of plan
  • Test from Apple is necessary
  • The owner can apply for auth token and auth coprocessor based on the "solution plan" with limitation, maybe 200-500 every month.

Mid-product

Propeller

The manufactory

Core Demands

I. Quick, the samples need to be ready in less than 3 months
II. Cheap, don't spend too much money before the order
III. Flexible, the product can satisfy different demands

Output

The product with full functions, which can be customed by the brand and distributor.

Mid-product

Problems

  • Too much work prescribed in the rule of product plan is useless for the situation
  • These work will cost a lot of money and time, and block the manufactory from HomeKit
    • Slower than others will increase the risk that the product might be out of fashion when it appears in the market
    • The manufactory has limited profitability, spending a lot of money and RD resources is very risky for them, the expect and stress to sell more products will be greater too

Mid-product

Suggestions

  • The idea is very similar to "solution plan", maybe there should be a "mid-product plan"
    • Based on hardware, functions, etc.
    • accessory self-certification is necessary, but the product shell can be replaced
    • packaging self-certification might be optional
  • The owner can apply for auth token and auth coprocessor based on the "mid-product plan" with limitation, maybe 10-50 every month.

End-product

End-product

Two Common Models

  • OEM

    The mid-product is ready, simple customization is enough

  • ODM

    The manufactory needs to develope the product from 0

End-product

Problems

  • The design of product plan is OK for these two models
  • But it's still a little bit slow for OEM

Suggestions

  • The self-certification for products based on "mid-product" might be simplified
  • There can be a relationship between "solution", "mid-product", "end-product"

Summary

  • Solution Plan

    Open to dev licensee
    Output the module with specific hardware
    The firmware function is not fixed

  • Mid-product Plan

    Output the product with specific PCBA and function
    The package and other certifications are not necessary
    The industrial design is not fixed
